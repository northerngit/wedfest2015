Food &...

This is probably the most important part of the day for Becky! We want to outline our plans for eating throughout the day, as a regular sit down three course meal isn't on the cards. Do not be alarmed however, we have plenty of delicious food available.

Please note: There will be a licenced bar serving drinks from 11am - 2am, there will be a vast selection of beverages including copious amounts of spirits and giant kegs of ales and cider.

Please be aware that this will be a CASH bar.

We do insist however that you please purchase all drinks from the bar - we are not insured for you to bring your own alcohol and would appreciate your support on this.

The drinks will be pub prices and we are in the process of planning 'happy hour' sessions on certain beverages during the day ...watch this space...!

Also if you are youthful enough to look under 25 then you may be asked for ID by the bar staff so make sure you bring appropriate ID with you on the day.

...Drink!

Saturday food:

Festival style food will be coming your way throughout the day with giant hot pans for lunch and a hog roast for dinner!

During the day we will host 'The great Wedfest bake off', where as many cakes as our guests can bake & bring with them will be served and sampled by everyone - and yes there will be a champion cake winner!

Please indicate on the RSVP if you are up for the challenge.

Sunday food:

Breakfast can be provided for all campers the next morning; consisting of a delicious cooked breakfast bap and hot drinks at the campsite... fivers at the ready please :-)

If you would like to 'opt in' for breakfast then please indicate this on the RSVP so we can plan accordingly.

We really hope everyone can stay around in the morning to reminisce and share fun memories of the night before.

Event Planning

Event Plans

Be prepared for a marathon dancing session with +6 bands throughout the day and night.

Get excited for fun activities and sports day games with a twist, as well as a 'Great Wedfest bake off' competition!

As well as crazy fun filled day there will be opportunities for relaxing with haybales, fire pits and chilled out, cosy corners to sit and chat.

A fully licenced paying bar from 11am - 2am.

Kit List

Flip flops or Wellies depending on the weather!! The marquee is in a farmers field - be wary ... heels may sink!

There is no dress code but we would encourage you to focus on the 'fest(ival)' more than the 'wed(ding)' style of dress.

Please see information in accommodation section for information on 'glamping' options. You may, of course, bring you own tent to pitch up in the camping field on the Saturday morning, from 9am.

Please bring your own sleeping bags, pillows and blow up mattresses (unless you specifically request them on the RSVP)

Running water, toilet blocks and electricity is onsite, so don't forget your toothbrush!

It is advised to prepare for all weathers as we are in the English countryside - so a rain jacket and suncream is recommended in your packing.

Please bear in mind that the evenings in August may still be chilly so a fleece/jumper is a wise idea for the evening sitting around the campfire till the early hours of the morning.

You will need ID and CASH for the bar and breakfast on Sunday.

Get your bespoke (non-glass) drinking vessels at the ready - tour, tour, tour ... I mean Wedfest!

Cameras! There is no way for us to photograph all the events of Wedfest 2015 without your help! Please bring your cameras and phones and take lots of pictures!
